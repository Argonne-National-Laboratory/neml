Mean effective stress
==========================

Overview
--------

This is the standard von Mises effective stress defined by

.. math::
   \sigma_e = \frac{\sigma_{11}+\sigma_{22}+\sigma_{33}}{3}

:math:`\bm{\sigma}` being the full stress tensor
